Bio

I am a visual designer and videographer with strengths in print design and videography using Adobe Creative Suite. Coming from a Taiwanese background, I value the idea of multiculturalism and teaching, including being a graphic design teaching assistant in Fraser International College and workshop facilitator in 'Techbytes', a program that teaches new designers programming languages and software in my university. Hence, I have several design experiences with organizations and programs who mainly work with international newcomers. During the design process, I am interested in doing research, content strategy, storytelling through sketching or illustrations, storyboarding and project management.

Goals

My current goal is to learn more about UX/UI design, web design (HTML/CSS) and game design (Java and Unity) that can potentially enrich myself as a designer. This website (coded by me) is one of my first web design projects and I am continuously learning more about this design field.
